Comparison of .png and .jpg

Why .png is better than .jpg

  • Ability to have a transparent background
  • Lossless compression, data is not lost when compressed.
  • Delivers higher quality prints with it's high resolution
  • Built in gamma correction so viewers can see the image the way it was intended
  • Can be interlaced, meaning with slow connection, the image will gradually raise in quality as it loads

    • Why .png is worse than .jpg

  • Because of lossless compression, file sizes tend to be larger
  • Not as suitable for high-resolution photography due to file size
  • Does not support animations
  • Less support on older programs
  • Can take up more storage space
